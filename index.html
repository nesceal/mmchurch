Malayang Mananampalataya Church

MALAYANG MANANAMPALATAYA CHURCH, INC. is a registered religious sect that originated in Morong, Rizal, Philippines since August 10, 1955.

The name Malayang Mananampalataya came from a consensus among the founders of this religion. The word "Malaya" refers to independence and freedom of faith and acceptance to anyone interested to be part of this religion regardless of race or religion they belong to provided they comply with the laws and policies of the church.

Introducing Himself in His worldly name, A.C.D.U.D.U.M. NIÑO DE PASTOR as the true Son of God, He wears a simple piece of cloth around His waist, one that symbolizes humility and appropriate for a Shepherd who will lead people to the path of their salvation by His good, true and practical teachings on righteousness.

History

HISTORY OF MALAYANG MANANAMPALATAYA CHURCH

  • THE BEGINNING

    In Maybancal, Morong, Rizal, Philippines, there lived a simple lady, Constantina T. San Antonio, known for her kind-heartedness, truthfulness and above all very pious indeed.She narrated that one day during a high school examination, she felt dizzy. Her kind teacher gave her first aid and a glass of water. When she regained her senses, Constantina recalled clearly that as she was trying to cope, her head tilted downward, someone in a long dress standing with bare feet caught her eyes and as she tried to look upward to see the face, she passed out.

  • THEOPHANY

    1955

    One day in July 1955, Constantina was washing clothes in the well about 10 meters away from their yard, feeling hungry she went up to the house alone. When she was eating she heard the call of whom she supposed was a beggar. She looked out and saw an old bearded man with a big cloth pouch tied on the cane he was holding. Constantina did not even had a cent to give as her step-mother went to the market. She was forced to ask for an excuse from the poor beggar. The beggar left followed by the eyes of Constantina and to her surprise, the beggar disappeared upon reaching the “is-is” tree just around five meters away from their house.

    It was August 10, 1955 when Constantina started to act strangely and preach in a peculiar manner not typical of herself until the second day He introduced himself as the Holy Spirit of God, the Son, with A.C.D.U.D.U.M. NIÑO DE PASTOR as his wordly name. His voice was small, coming deep from within.

  • The family mistook it as a sign of insanity and feared that, as bunch of listeners gathered to listen to her continuous preaching without sleep and lack of food she might be ill that she was brought to the doctor who gave her sleeping pills. Constantina took the pills intended for three(3) days in one (1) dosage. She never slept and continued preaching. The family brought her an old herbalist/faith healer who performed spiritual healing with the use of alum wherein the picture of an old man with a bread transformed from the liquefied alum. Later, another faith healer, tied a hanky on the forehead of Constantina saying if the spirit in her is evil, she would remove it, otherwise, good spirit is reigning. The latter happened. Constantina continued preaching with the hanky on her forehead and the people who gathered to listen to her teachings came in huge quantity.

  • THE APPARITION OF THE VIRGIN MARY, MOTHER OF MERCY

    In May of 1956, at 4 o'clock in the morning, Constantina clearly saw the image of the Blessed Virgin Mary on top of the avocado tree near their house. A few minutes later, according to her, the image disappeared from her sight.

  • ACCEPTANCE

    More and more people came to listen to Constantina about good deeds and good manners. Some of them just wanted to listen while others wanted a cure for their illnesses. There are those who just wanted to seek lost items, some others asked for solutions for their problems. Wherever she went, people followed her even at the Parish church when she preached beside the Holy Nazarene.

    The people observed the change in her voice, appearance, and behavior whenever the spirit is in her. He called people by their real names and sometimes other names seemingly the names that they should have had. “Tancing” was how He called Constantina.

  • REJECTION

    She kept preaching, treating the sick day and night, until the Parish Priest of the church declared rejection in one of his sermons, telling the people not to believe Constantina’s words because she is insane. The people believed the priest criticized and ridiculed Constantina.

    “You know what the Priest did to us while praying near the Holy Nazarene at the church? We were dragged towards the door and sent us out,”, according to one of the followers of Constantina who happened to be one of the founders.

  • THE SIGNIFICANT ACT

    The actions and remarks of the Parish Priest hurt the family and those who believed in the Spirit. Because of this, seven hundred seventy two (772) of her followers, then decided to organize a religious group separate from the Roman Catholic or any other existing religions.

    That step made Malayang Mananampalataya Church an independent Christian religion that is now known to exist from 1955 to the present, whose sacraments and teachings came from the Holy Spirit of the Son of God, A.C.D.U.D.U.M. NIÑO DE PASTOR, the savior of mankind.

Beliefs

THE EYE

Three persons in one God - the Father, the Son, and the Holy Spirit. God the Father is watching over his creatures and nothing can be hidden from Him, bad and good deeds. God, the Son descended to Earth to preach righteousness and lead people to the right path of salvation. God, the Holy Spirit guides people to what is true, what is right and what is good.

A.C.D.U.D.U.M. NIÑO DE PASTOR

The worldly name of the spirit of the true Son of God who descended to earth to spread His teachings on righteousness and lead mankind to the path of salvation.

VIRGIN MARY, MOTHER OF MERCY

She symbolizes women. She is the Light that guides us and intercedes for us in our Lord so that we can be forgiven of our sins.

FAITH

True faith is the right practice of commandments and teachings of God that a religion implements to its followers. A Godly person has a strong will to suppress the weak emotions in order to resist evil and instead, do good deeds besides being pious and knows how to play along with the different behavior of other people. Our Lord wants us to be practical and simple with our faith, not lavish.

CONSCIENCE

Conscience is a grain of power that God endowed in each of His creatures so that He can faithfully judge what man thinks and does, whether good or bad. The conscience is a small voice inside us and tells us whether our deeds are right or wrong.

CONFESSION

We do not confess our sins to a priest. The proper confession is to ask forgiveness to the person whom you committed a sin and to Him.

GOOD FRIDAY

The true meaning of Good Friday is to remember the teachings of the Lord Jesus Christ and to repent of the sins and trying not to repeat the same sins again. Fasting and physically hurting oneself will not make up for and redeem our sins. We do not restrict ourselves to taking a bath and eating meat on the season of lent.

CHRISTMAS

The meaning of Christmas is love, giving,, mutual support. forgiveness and should be done not only during the holidays but every day. December 15 is the Christmas celebration of our church because according to A.C.D.U.D.U.M. NIÑO DE PASTOR, that is the date He descended to earth.

PURGATORY AND HELL

A.C.D.U.D.U.M clearly stated that there is a place for screening the souls for what they did during their life on earth, good or bad. Thus, there is a place and process for screening. Hell is not a place but a situation or condition here on earth and even in the afterlife. It means problems like poverty in money, disease, etc.
